Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any rules?

Yes there are! You can find them on our rules page.

What should we bring?

You should bring your computer, charger, money, and yourself!

Is this a hackathon?

No. This is a problem-based computer science competition.

How much will it cost?

The competition itself costs nothing.

Do I need previous coding experience?

No. Anyone with any experience level can compete.

Do I need a team?

Teams are not necesary, but a team is beneficial. Teams consist of 2-4 members and in addition to the individual rounds, they also participate in the team rounds.
